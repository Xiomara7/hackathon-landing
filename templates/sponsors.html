SPONSORSHIP PACKAGES

Who are we.

#include<girls> is a student driven non-profit organization that encourages women in Science, Technology Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).

What is this about.

Our event, #include<a-thon> is a hackathon that gathers Puerto Rico's most talented students in STEM for a weekend to hack, build and collaborate on software and hardware ideas.

Our Goal.

Our goal is to produce a hackathon evenly balanced in gender, maintain/increase the quality of hacks and take down the stereotypes that exist mostly in STEM fields.

Why should you sponsor.

By participating as a sponsor, your organization will benefit from extended exposure through media campaigns and promotional efforts directed toward the supporters of #include<girls>.

About Include Girls

#Include<girls> is a student driven non-profit organization from the UPR for women in STEM. Our goal is to outreach not only women, but a minority represented in our science.

We don't segregate ourselves, we support diversity. We also aim to change the erroneous image that the students can have about Computer Science.
