Why a Hackathon?

We aim to produce a hackathon evenly balanced in gender while maintaining and increasing the quality of hacks. We want to motivate women to show off their skills and ideas for new projects.

Why a Fashion Show?

We want to take down the stereotypes that exist in our field. Instead of male dominated hobbies we want to come up with a hobby highly classified as a female hobby to show how diverse hackathons can be.

Why a Job Fair?

This will be the first Job Fair ever held exclusively for Computer Science/Computer Engineering students from different colleges all over the island. We want to provide participants with a network of companies for possible internship/job opportunities.

But, what is a Hackathon?

#inlcude <a-thon> is an event where people with technical skills come together for 24 hours to create amazing projects. These projects are then judged by a group of tech professionals and winners will possibly win cool prizes.Click here to learn more.

Can I have teamates?

The teams can have from 2 to 4 members. Since we're trying to promote women to participate the teams MOST have at least 1 women. Each team must bring their own computers. Teams are recommended to have at least 1 programmer, but it’s not mandatory.

Who can participate?

We welcome college students from any university and major. High school students can participate if they're 18 or older. We also welcome proffessionals to participate if they have graduate this year. See the Code of Conduct.

Our goal is to produce a hackathon evenly balanced in gender, maintain/increase the quality of hacks and take down the stereotypes that exist mostly in our field.

About Include Girls

#Include<girls> is a student driven non-profit organization from the UPR for women in STEM. Our goal is to outreach not only women, but a minority represented in our science.

We don't segregate ourselves, we support diversity. We also aim to change the erroneous image that the students can have about Computer Science.
