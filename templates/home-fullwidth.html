Why a Hackathon?

We want to promote women to participate in technical events and motivate them to show off their skills and ideas for new projects.

Why a Fashion Show?

We're trying to break the stereotype thinking that programers can't be into Fashion either!

Why a Job Fair?

We want that all our participants have access to network with companies and have the opportunity for possible hire.

But, what is a Hackathon?

#inlcude "A_THON" is an event where people with technical skills come together for 24 hours to create amazing projects. These projects are then judge by a group of tech proffessionals for the change to win cool prizes. Click here to learn more

Can I have teamates?

The teams can have from 2 to 4 members. Since we're trying to promote women to participate the teams MOST have at least 2 women in each team. Each team must bring their own computers. Programmers are recommended in each team but not mandatory.

Who can participate?

We welcome college students from any university and degree. High school students can participate if they're 18 or older. We also welcome proffessionals to participate. For the people who are going to participate in the hackathon the admission is free.

About Include Girls

We are a group of students from the University of Puerto Rico, wanting to offer support and guidance to women interested in Computer Science.

Our goal is to outreach not only women, but a minority represented in our science. We don't segregate ourselves, we support diversity. We also aim to change the erroneous image that the students can have about Computer Science.
