Rules

  • Teams may include up to four members

  • All hacks must be built at #include<a-thon> by registered attendees

  • Remote participation is not allowed

  • A participant can belong to just one group

  • Project contents must be general audience rated

  • Everyone must be identified at all times

  • Anyone must abide the MLH Code of Conduct

    About Include Girls

    #Include<girls> is a student driven non-profit organization from the UPR for women in STEM. Our goal is to outreach not only women, but a minority represented in our science.

    We don't segregate ourselves, we support diversity. We also aim to change the erroneous image that the students can have about Computer Science.
