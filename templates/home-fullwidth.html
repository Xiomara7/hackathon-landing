Why a Hackathon?

We aim to produce a hackathon evenly balanced in gender while maintaining and increasing the quality of hacks. We want to motivate women to show off their skills and ideas for new projects.

Why a Fashion Show?

We want to take down the stereotypes that exist in our field that programmers can’t be into fashion. Instead of male dominated hobbies we want to come up with a hobby highly classified as a female hobby to show how diverse hackathons can be.

Why a Job Fair?

This will be the first job fair ever held exclusively for Computer Science/Computer Engineering students from different colleges all over the island. We want to provide participants with a network of companies for possible internship/job opportunities.

But, what is a Hackathon?

#inlcude <a-thon> is an event where people with technical skills come together for 24 hours to create amazing projects. These projects are then judged by a group of tech professionals and winners will possibly win cool prizes. Click here to learn more.

Can I have teamates?

The teams can have from 2 to 4 members. Since we're trying to promote women to participate the teams MUST have at least 1 woman. Each team must bring their own computers. Teams are recommended to have at least 1 programmer, but it’s not mandatory.

Who can participate?

We welcome college students from any university and major. High school students can participate if they're 18 or older. We also welcome professionals to participate if they have graduated this year. See the Code of Conduct.

Do you give travel reimbursement?

Unfortunately, we’re not offering any travel reimbursement for people outside Puerto Rico at the moment. We will make an announcement if we’re able to offer any in the near future.

Can I stay over?

If you’re going to participate in the hackathon, you can stay over. If you want, you can bring a sleeping bag, air mattress, pillows, etc. We will provide rooms for the participants that would like to rest.

Is the admission free?

For the students that are planning to come to the Job Fair and the Hackathon, the admission is free and it includes the Fashion Show.

About Include Girls

#Include<girls> is a student driven non-profit organization from the UPR for women in STEM. Our goal is to outreach not only women, but a minority represented in our science.

We don't segregate ourselves, we support diversity. We also aim to change the erroneous image that the students can have about Computer Science.
